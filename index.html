Support

Roofing Expert is the carrier-ready roof inspection report app for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch — built by a roofer with 30 years in the field.

Need help?

Email Adam.Fischer@icloud.com and include what you were doing, what you expected, and what happened instead. Screenshots help.

Common questions

Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. See the Privacy Policy — the short version is: we never see your data.

A report shows red flags — is something wrong? No. Flags mark fields that are still empty (like a claim number that hasn't been issued yet). Reports always save; flags clear as you fill things in.

Photos aren't GPS-stamped. Allow Location access (While Using) in Settings → Roofing Expert.
